I’m Courtney, a Full Stack Software Engineer!

It’s exciting to say out loud because I recently completed a certificate program for just that, at Thinkful. I learned how to be a good Developer with a love/hate appreciation for code - I think that is required.

I fell in love with the consistent learning, then out of love with the bugs that made me want to pull my hair out, then back in love when I found the bug, and rinse.repeat. It’s the most frustrating, satisfying, creative skill I’ve ever possessed, and that is what makes it so interesting.

Fun Facts

Outside of coding I’m an outdoorsman at heart. Growing up in Oregon I was raised with beautiful scenery, and though the places I’ve lived have been beautiful - Hawaii, San Diego, North Carolina - nothing compares to the Pacific Northwest.

I try to spend as much time out in nature as I can, and my little dog Yahoo accompanies me on many hikes. I plan to one day hike the Pacific Crest Trail, though I imagine I’d probably have to carry Yahoo for most of the way - I think we would both be fine with that.

Coffee, Tea, Matcha - I like all the caffeine. Give me that and a good book and I’m all set. Bonus points if this in a hammock in the woods.
